features

A whole workstation, in the prompt.

Everything you'd open ten tabs for — local, scriptable, and yours.

[ Chat & Agents ]

Multi-turn chat and autonomous agents that read, plan, and edit your repo.

[ Tools & MCP ]

Bring any tool over the Model Context Protocol. Wire up your own in minutes.

[ Local Models ]

Point at Ollama, llama.cpp, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. No data leaves the box.

[ Research ]

Fetch, read, and summarize across sources without breaking your flow.

[ Documents ]

Drop in PDFs and notes; ask questions grounded in your own files.

[ Themes ]

Restyle every pixel. This very page is one theme — make it yours.
